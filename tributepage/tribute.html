King Prithivi Narayan Shah

The man who who unites Nepal

Here's a timeline of Prithivi Narayan Shah's life in A.D.:

  • 1723 - Born in the Royal Family of Gorkha, Nepal
  • 1743 -Became the king of his state at the small age of 21 yrs after his father death.
  • 1744 -After training his armies and producing weapons, started attacking neighbouring states and conquered Nuwakot.
  • 1766 - Conquered Kirtipur on his third attempt.
  • 1768 - Attacked Kathmandu in the occasion of Indrajatra and attack Patan and Bhaktapur and conquered.
  • 1769 -made Kathmandu valley the capital city of Nepal and started to unite eastern states of Nepal
  • 1775 - The great king runs come to an end and he left the world at the age of 52 yrs
Upon his death, Prithivi Narayan Shah was succeeeded by his son Pratab Singh Shah
